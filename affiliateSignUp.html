Nice Traders Affiliate Program

The NICE Traders program is intended to not only save travelers money but also reward those who help get travelers into the program. All rewards are based on contributions to the company and are paid completely (and only) on results. No one can buy themselves into a position of seniority; nor does anyone have to pay any money to become an earning member.

NICE Agents

The success of NICE Traders depends on people all over the country, and in fact all over the world, signing up and posting their foreign currency for exchange. To facilitate people learning about the site, we have set up a very generous affiliates program. Anyone can be affiliated with NICE Traders and with absolutely minimal investment they can earn money for years to come.

The most “hands on“ affiliation is a NICE Agent. When individuals register with NICE Traders to be an affiliate, they are given their own QR code and website link that identifies them as a NICE Agent. When a person scans the QR code or enters the link they will be directed to our website. When that individual signs up with NICE Traders, they are registered as a NICE Traveler and is a client of the referring NICE Agent. Whenever that Traveler completes a currency exchange with another Traveler, the NICE Agents for each Traveler receives $.30 of the commission. That is 30% of the gross revenue received by the company. That’s more than profit sharing. It’s income sharing!

The NICE Agent will be provided with suggested ways of attracting Travelers, and there will be almost no expense the Agent needs to incur. The Agent can contact their friends and social media contacts as well as family and neighbors. Similarly, they can put up fliers on bulletin boards and simply copy his QR code onto the flyer or business cards. The cost of printer paper and ink is the extent of what an agent would be required to pay. Of course, they could have business cards printed with their QR code and site link. Otherwise, the investment is simply time, and, since they earn money every time one of their Traveler makes a trade, they have the opportunity to earn money for many years to come.

NICE Managers

To encourage travelers to become Agents, there is a wide range of groups that we reach out to: college students, military members and retirees, church groups, businessmen, etc. are filled with people who travel and can benefit from saving money. NICE Managers are affiliates who specifically recruit Agents into the program. When an Agent signs a Traveler who makes a trade, the Agent receives the 30% commission, and the NICE Manager who recruited the Agent, receives $0.10. As the NICE Manager recruits numerous Agents and the Agents garner Travelers, everyone makes money and Travelers save money.

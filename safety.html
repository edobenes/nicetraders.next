Safety

Safety of all travelers is paramount. While we can’t guarantee safety, we encourage awareness and planning when arranging your trade. Our best recommendations are:

Meet at a public place! We provide a police station, fire department, and/or a library location within your search radius.

Ask questions before you meet. . Inquiring about their travels may provide confirmation and comfort prior to meeting in person. Deal locally, face-to-face —follow this one rule and avoid 99% of scam attempts.

Do not provide payment to anyone you have not met in person.

Don't accept cashier/certified checks or money orders - banks cash counterfeits then hold you responsible.

Transactions are between users only, no third party provides a "guarantee".

Never give out financial or personal information (bank account, social security, dates of travel, paypal account, etc).
